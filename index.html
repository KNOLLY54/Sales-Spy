Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is your data?

Our data is updated daily and verified through multiple sources. We use advanced algorithms and manual checks to ensure the highest level of accuracy. However, as with any data service, there might be occasional discrepancies. We're constantly improving our systems to minimize these occurrences.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to the service until the end of your current billing period. We don't offer refunds for partial months of service.

How do you collect this data?

We collect data from various public sources, including store websites, social media profiles, and other publicly available information. We use a combination of web scraping, API integrations, and partnerships with data providers to gather and verify our data.
